About the Documentary

This groundbreaking documentary explores the rich history and architectural marvels of the Khmer Empire, which ruled much of Southeast Asia from the 9th to the 15th centuries.

Through stunning cinematography and expert interviews, we uncover the secrets behind Angkor Wat and other magnificent temples, revealing the advanced engineering, artistic achievements, and spiritual depth of this ancient civilization.

Join us on a journey through time as we piece together the story of one of history's most remarkable empires.

Documentary Features

Ancient Architecture

Explore the engineering marvels of Khmer temples and urban planning that still astonish architects today.

Hydraulic System

Discover the sophisticated water management system that sustained the empire's agricultural prosperity.

Lost History

Uncover the reasons behind the empire's decline and the mysteries that remain unsolved.
